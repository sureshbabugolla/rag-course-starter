================================================================================
RETURN POLICY
================================================================================

Customers may return any product within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
Items must be in their original condition with all original packaging included.
Products that have been damaged, used excessively, or modified are not eligible
for return.

To initiate a return, visit our returns portal at acme.com/returns or send an
email to support@acme.com with your order number and reason for return.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.
Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

For defective items, we offer an immediate replacement or full refund including
original shipping costs. Photos of the defect may be required.

PRODUCT WARRANTY
================================================================================

All ACME products come with a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing
defects and hardware failures under normal use conditions.

The warranty does not cover:
- Physical damage caused by drops, spills, or accidents
- Water damage beyond the product's rated water resistance
- Normal wear and tear
- Damage from unauthorized modifications or repairs
- Consumable parts such as batteries unless damage has occurred due to a defect

To file a warranty claim, email support@acme.com with:
1. Proof of purchase (order number or receipt)
2. Description of the defect
3. Photos or video of the issue

Warranty claims are reviewed within 2 business days. Approved claims receive
either a repair, replacement, or refund at ACME's discretion.

Extended warranty plans are available for purchase within 30 days of buying
a product. The ACME Care+ plan extends coverage to 3 years and includes
accidental damage protection for $29.99/year.

ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
================================================================================

Creating an account: Visit acme.com/signup and enter your email and password.
You will receive a verification email — click the link within 24 hours to activate.

Forgotten password: Go to acme.com/reset-password and enter your email address.
A reset link will be sent within 5 minutes. Check your spam folder if not received.

Changing your email address: Log in and go to Account Settings > Personal Info.
You will need to verify the new email address before the change takes effect.

Deleting your account: Send a request to support@acme.com. Accounts are
deactivated within 48 hours. Personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
in compliance with our privacy policy. Order history is retained for 7 years
for legal and tax purposes.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?
A: Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement by contacting
support via live chat or phone. After 1 hour, the order enters processing and
cannot be changed, but you may return it once received.

Q: Do you offer student or military discounts?
A: Yes! Students get 15% off with a valid .edu email address. Military personnel
and veterans receive 20% off with verified ID. Apply at acme.com/discounts.

Q: Is my payment information stored securely?
A: ACME does not store full credit card numbers. All payments are processed
through Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 compliant — the highest level of
payment security certification.

Q: Do you have a physical store?
A: ACME is an online-only retailer. We do not have physical retail locations.
However, our products are available at select partner retailers including
Best Buy and Amazon.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Tracking information is emailed to you within 1 hour of shipment. You can
also track your order at acme.com/track using your order number.
